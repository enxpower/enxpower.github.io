Energy & Power Infrastructure

AGI&M ASSETS INC. is a hardware-focused operating company delivering energy and power infrastructure for infrastructure-grade deployments across North America.

What We Do

We support mission-critical infrastructure projects with a focus on reliability, traceability, and disciplined execution. Our work spans energy storage systems, power and grid infrastructure, and data center power interconnect hardware.

Core Capabilities

  • Energy storage system hardware for commercial and industrial applications
  • Power and grid infrastructure components and system integration support
  • Data center power interconnect hardware for hyperscale and AI environments

How We Work

Our delivery model emphasizes controlled scope, documented interfaces, and long-term operational stability. We operate through program-based execution and collaborate closely with EPCs, system integrators, and infrastructure partners.

Markets Served

  • Commercial and industrial energy infrastructure
  • Utility and EPC-led infrastructure deployments
  • Hyperscale and AI data center environments
