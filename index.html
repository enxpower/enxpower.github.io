Energy & Power Infrastructure

AGI&M ASSETS INC. is a hardware-focused operating company delivering energy and power infrastructure for mission-critical deployments across North America.

What We Do

We support infrastructure-grade projects with a focus on reliability, traceability, and execution. Our work spans energy storage systems, power and grid infrastructure, and data center power interconnects.

Core Capabilities

- Energy storage systems for commercial and industrial applications - Power and grid infrastructure components and integration - Data center power interconnect hardware for hyperscale environments

How We Work

Our approach emphasizes controlled scope, documented delivery, and long-term operational stability. We operate through program-based execution and collaborate closely with EPCs, integrators, and infrastructure partners.

Markets Served

- Commercial and industrial energy projects - Utility and EPC-led infrastructure deployments - Hyperscale and AI data center environments
