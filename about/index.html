About

AGI&M ASSETS INC. is a Canada-based operating company focused on the delivery of energy and power infrastructure hardware.

Company Profile

Founded in 2019, AGI&M ASSETS INC. operates as a hardware-focused organization supporting infrastructure-grade projects across North America. Our work emphasizes disciplined execution, documented scope, and long-term operational reliability.

Operating Model

We deliver through program-based execution models designed to support repeatable outcomes and clear interface definitions. Our role centers on hardware delivery, supply coordination, and integration readiness within broader project ecosystems.

Scope and Boundaries

AGI&M ASSETS INC. operates as a hardware-focused operating company. Software platforms and intellectual property are developed and licensed separately. This separation ensures clarity of responsibility and alignment with project and regulatory requirements.

Commitment

We prioritize reliability, traceability, and controlled delivery in every engagement. Our objective is to support infrastructure partners with dependable hardware programs that integrate cleanly into complex project environments.
