About

AGI&M ASSETS INC. is a Canada-based operating company focused on the delivery of energy and power infrastructure hardware.

Company Profile

Founded in 2019, AGI&M ASSETS INC. operates as a hardware-focused organization supporting infrastructure-grade projects across North America.

Operating Model

We deliver through program-based execution models designed to support repeatable outcomes and clear interface definitions, centered on disciplined hardware delivery.

Scope and Boundaries

AGI&M ASSETS INC. operates as a hardware-focused operating company. Software platforms and intellectual property are developed and licensed separately to ensure clarity of responsibility and alignment with regulatory requirements.

Commitment

We prioritize reliability, traceability, and controlled delivery in every engagement, supporting infrastructure partners with dependable hardware programs.
