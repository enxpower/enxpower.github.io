Programs

AGI&M ASSETS INC. structures its work through defined programs rather than ad-hoc product offerings. Each program represents a controlled scope of hardware delivery aligned with specific infrastructure use cases.

Program-Based Execution

Programs are designed to support repeatable execution, clear interfaces, and documented delivery. This enables consistency across projects while allowing adaptation to site-specific requirements.

Typical Program Scope

A program may include:

  • Defined hardware scope and technical specifications
  • Supply chain coordination and documentation
  • Integration readiness for EPCs or system integrators
  • Deployment context and operational considerations

Current Focus Areas

Our active programs support:

  • Energy storage systems
  • Power and grid infrastructure
  • Data center power interconnects

Program details are documented separately to ensure clarity and traceability.
