Capabilities

AGI&M ASSETS INC. provides hardware-focused capabilities across energy and power infrastructure. Our offerings are structured to support program-based delivery for infrastructure-grade projects.

Energy Storage Systems

We support the delivery of energy storage system hardware for commercial and industrial applications. Our scope focuses on system-level hardware, integration readiness, and deployment alignment with project requirements.

Power and Grid Infrastructure

We deliver power and grid infrastructure components that support safe and reliable interconnection. This includes electrical distribution hardware and balance-of-system elements used in regulated environments.

Data Center Power Interconnects

We provide power interconnect hardware designed for data center environments. Our work supports rack-level and system-level power delivery with an emphasis on reliability and operational stability.

Delivery Approach

Capabilities are executed through controlled programs with documented scope, traceable supply chains, and clear interface definitions. This approach enables consistent delivery across diverse infrastructure projects.
